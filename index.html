about us

THE FOUNDATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF VIETNAM TOURISM

Established in 2020 amid the increasing demand for tourism, cultural and historical learning in the world, but due to the prolonged epidemic situation, people cannot leave the area. HKN Tour appears on the domestic and international tourism market with the motto of home travel with high quality images, surprisingly low prices. From domestic museums, stretching from North to South to famous museums in the world, spread from all continents. HKN Tour is always ready to serve all customers, all walks of life, with the common purpose of being safe, convenient, and exciting when experiencing travel at home.

Developed over 2000 years of history. Each ethnic group, each country, and each continent have their own culture, civilization and long history. It is equally important to learn and study history for each person in each host country. How can people have enough time to condense, learn so much knowledge through more than 2000 years of history, as well as more than 4 continents and more than 100 countries around the world.

Understanding the importance as well as the learning and learning needs of each individual in this period, the company was established to synthesize information from museums around the world, providing users with the most practical content and images but still keeping the highlights in each culture. Joining us, you have enjoyable and safe experiences during the time when the disease is spreading around the world.
